## Season overview

When you select a season from the home page, you'll see a complete list of all races for that year. Each race in the list displays:

- **Round number** - The sequential race number in the season
- **Race name** - The official name of the Grand Prix
- **Date** - When the race takes place
- **Circuit** - The name of the track

Upcoming races are marked with an **UPCOMING** tag to help you quickly identify which races haven't happened yet.

<Tip>
The season list shows races in chronological order, making it easy to track the progression of the championship.
</Tip>

## Race details

Click on any race to view comprehensive information about that event.

### Race header

The top of each race page shows:

- Round number and season year
- Race name and circuit
- Location (city and country)
- Date and time in your local timezone
- Link to Wikipedia for additional race information
- Quick links to view the same round in other seasons

<Note>
Race times are automatically converted to your local timezone for convenience.
</Note>

### Grid and results

The results table displays complete information for every driver:

- **Finish position** - Final race placement
- **Driver** - Driver name with three-letter code, linked to their profile
- **Constructor** - Team name, linked to constructor profile
- **Grid position** - Starting position on the grid
- **Status** - Race completion status (Finished, DNF reason, etc.)
- **Points** - Championship points earned

#### Position changes

Next to each finish position, you'll see indicators showing how many positions a driver gained or lost:

- **Green arrows** - Positions gained from grid to finish
- **Red arrows** - Positions lost from grid to finish

Drivers who set the fastest lap of the race have an **FL** tag displayed next to their name.

<Tip>
On smaller screens, the results automatically switch to a card layout for easier viewing.
</Tip>

#### Viewing all results

By default, the results table shows the top 5 finishers. Click **Show more** to expand the full grid and see all entries including retirements.

### Championship standings

Below the race results, you'll find championship standings tables showing how the race affected the title fight.

<Note>
Standings are only displayed for races that have been completed and synced.
</Note>
